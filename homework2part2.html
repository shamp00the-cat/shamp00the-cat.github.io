Homework 2, Part 2

Reflection:

Once again, as I hope you can see, I'm playing with how to make media evoke an eery feeling. There are subject matters, such as a doorway that you can't see past, which subconsiously read as somewhat dangerous. Here, I wanted to see what kinds of lighting and colors evoke the same feeling. I feel like the more texture there is to the photo, the more dirty it appears, and the harder it is to relax when looking at it. Colors like green, to me, can be sickly or dirty, while red can be threatening. There isn't a monster or anything in these photos; in real life I see these places everyday and they are very neutral, comfortable even. Yet, when shot a ceratin way and manipulated another, they can become anxiety inducing in the same way that a shaking camera in a film can affect the viewer.
