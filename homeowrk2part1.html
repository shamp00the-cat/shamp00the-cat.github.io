Reflection:

I went into this assignment admittedly confused about depth of field, and how to acheive it. I knew what kind of pictures I wanted to use the effect on, but I had a hard time understanding how to successfully maneuver the camera. I grow to have more and more respect for photographers the more I learn about cameras. Something that seems so accessible and intuitive can quickly become more complex and difficult that you'd imagined! That being said, I'm very drawn to portals as subject matter in film. Hallways, doorways, stairwells are all very interesting to me. Capturing these while there are people moving through them was really fun, and a concept I plan to build on as time goes on. I think it could be worth it to use shallow or deep depth of field depending on what kind of feeling you are trying to evoke. Something I didn't fully grasp until after I used the camera and came back to lab was how to adjust the light. That is something I need to work on.
