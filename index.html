Personal Creative Statement:

WHO I AM:

I'm an aspiring web designer. I have experience with, but am always learning more about: software engineering, web development, UI/UX design, and digital media production. I bring insight and experience from both the visual side of design and the more technical programming aspects of development for a nuanced, holistic approach. I love designing, editing, and coding on my free time. I also love playing CTF's and dabbling in penetration testing. I am passionate about all things web, code, design, and media.

WHERE I DRAW INSPIRATION:

I've always been very interested in film and visual arts, and where they meet. My favorite director is David Lynch; I find his avant-garde style of film making very inspiring. I'm inspired by films/shows that you could pause at any moment, and the image is so beautiful you could justifyably print and frame it. David Lynch combines beautiful imagery and composition with amazing original soundtracks that create a cinematic world that hypnotizes viewers and suck them into the experience. I would love to build on my basic videography and photo/video editing skills, to see if there's anything I could express through that medium. I feel that filmmaking gives the ultimate creative outlet as it combines writing, story telling, visual art and music.
