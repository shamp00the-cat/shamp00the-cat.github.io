Personal Creative Statement:

I've always been very interested in film and visual arts, and where they meet. My favorite director is David Lynch; I find his avant-garde style of film making very inspiring. I love a movie or show that you could pause at any moment, and the image is so beautiful you could justifyably print and frame it. David Lynch combines beautiful imagery and composition with amazing original soundtracks that create a cinematic world that hypnotizes viewers, like a dream. I would love to learn the basic skills of film making such as camera skills and photo/video editing, to see if there's anything I could express through that medium. I feel that filmmaking gives the ultimate creative outlet as it combines writing, story telling, visual art and music. I am also an aspiring web developer, and I hope to grow my basic web design skills in this class.
